# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
French small business owners (TPE/PME), artisans, and local commerce managers. These are traditional B2B profiles who are very short on time (55% state this as their main constraint). They are looking for simplicity, reassurance, and concrete ROI (saving time, capturing leads, complying with regulations like electronic invoicing).

## Product Purpose
A showcase website for a solo digital agency providing Web infrastructure, SEO, Process Automation (BPA), and AI integration. The primary goal is to convert visitors into booking a free 15-to-30-minute strategic discovery call / digital audit to qualify their needs.

## Brand Personality
Pragmatique, Expert, Transparent.
The feel is "artisanat numérique" (digital craftsmanship) – similar to independent experts like Vlad Cerisier or Paul Vengeons. It focuses on transparency, high-value technical content, and ultra-detailed case studies showing concrete ROI over standard marketing rhetoric.

## Anti-references
- No marketing jargon, "Growth Hacking" buzzwords, excessive anglicisms, or generic corporate communication tropes.
- No "black box" formats with vague service descriptions or opaque subscription models.
- No performance killers: avoid heavy animations, complex scroll-jacking, or unoptimized assets that degrade site speed and Core Web Vitals.

## Design Principles
- **Cognitive Clarity First:** The layout must allow time-poor business owners to capture the value proposition and take action in seconds.
- **Show Concrete Value:** Focus on detailed case studies, transparency, and ROI rather than vague promises.
- **Frictionless and Fast:** Clean typography, high contrast, seamless mobile responsive UX, and impeccable Core Web Vitals. No visual or technical friction.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
High-contrast elements, clean typography, and a seamless mobile responsive UX are required. While strict WCAG AAA certification is not legally mandated, the site must strictly follow web performance and accessibility clean coding standards for ease of reading by a non-technical audience.
